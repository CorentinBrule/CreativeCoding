Most Common Questions

I am a home delivery customer. How do I activate my free digital access?
Close

Print subscribers to The New York Times and The International New York Times get free access to NYTimes.com, plus the full range of NYTimes apps. If your home delivery account is already connected to the account you use to read and comment on NYTimes.com, you won't need to do a thing. If not, you'll just need to connect your home delivery account to an NYTimes.com account.
