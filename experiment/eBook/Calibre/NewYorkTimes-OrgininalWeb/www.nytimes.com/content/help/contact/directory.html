Customer Care

Account Management

Need help with your subscription?

  • Manage your account online at nytimes.com/myaccount.
  • To temporarily suspend delivery of your newspapers, visit homedelivery.nytimes.com. You can also report missing or damaged papers here, and we'll provide you with a replacement or credit as soon as possible.
  • Visit our Help section to find solutions to many common problems.

Couldn't solve your issue?

  • Contact us using our help form. Home delivery subscribers should contact us here, while digital subscribers should use this form.
  • To change or cancel your subscription, please call us. United States subscribers should call 1-800-698‑4637. For subscribers outside the United States, we have many local contact numbers; please see our international contact information.
  • Tweet at us @nytsupport with your product and service questions.

If you receive home delivery of The International New York Times, please visit this page.

Technical Issues

To report a technical issue with our website or apps, email our tech team and include as much detail as possible.

Corrections

For a possible correction, or to reach the website's editorial staff, you can send an email.

News Departments and Reporters

  • To send comments and suggestions (about news coverage only) or to report errors that call for a correction, email us or leave a message at 1‑844‑NYT‑NEWS (1‑844‑698‑6397).
  • Most reporters can be reached directly by clicking the byline of any article by that reporter. This will take you to the reporter's Times Topics page, where you can click the Send an Email to... link to access the contact form.

Advertising

  • For the print reservations procedure and material requirements visit our Self-Service Advertising Portal or call (212) 556-7777.
  • For information about advertising on NYTimes.com, visit nytmediakit.com.
  • Send an email to the Senior V.P. of Advertising.

Listings: Classifieds, Notices, and Announcements

  • To place a classified ad, death notice or other listing, go to www.nytimes.com/ref/classifieds/.
  • For information about classified advertising, visit advertising.nytimes.com/classified or call 1‑800‑AD‑TIMES (1‑800‑238‑4637).

Wedding/Celebration Announcements

  • For information about submitting a wedding or celebration announcement, or to submit an announcement, visit listings.nytimes.com/Weddings. If you have additional questions, you can also email us or call (212) 556‑7325.
  • To obtain a high-quality reprint of a wedding or celebration announcement that appeared in the Times, please call PARS International at (212) 221‑9595, extension 210, or visit www.nytreprints.com to order online.

Purchase NYTimes Products

  • For information on The New York Times Replica Edition, go here.
  • For information regarding the The Times Digest and the Evening Digest, please call us at (212) 556‑1200, email us at timesdigest@nytimes.com or visit www.timesdigest.com.

Education Initiatives of The New York Times

The New York Times in Education: Get reduced rates for home or school delivery to college students, faculty and K-12 educators, a newspaper readership program, teaching strategies for using The Times in the classroom and more.

To subscribe at the education rate, visit NYTimes.com/eduoffers.

For college and university resources, visit NYTimes.com/edu or send an email to edu@nytimes.com.

For K-12 resources, visit NYTimes.com/nie or send an email to schools@nytimes.com.
